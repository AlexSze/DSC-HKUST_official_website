Privacy Policy

In accordance with the “Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance”, DSC@HKUST adopts the following policy:
1. To ensure that web users’ personal data are secure, DSC@HKUST shall not actively disclose any personal data without consent and shall protect such data with maximum effort.
2. As you access this website, internet providers may log your data, such as domain name, IP addresses and accessed webpages, on their servers. These data are exclusively used for statistical purposes and cannot be used to identify users.
3. Any personal data entrusted with DSC@HKUST shall be handled in accordance with principles of security and privacy. Such data shall exclusively be used for storage and operational purposes.
4. DSC@HKUST welcomes direct contact via social media as listed in the “Contact Us” section.
5. Information enclosed, if any, shall only be used to reply to received messages.
6. DSC@HKUST shall not disclose any personal data to third parties without user’s consent.
